What are Prepositions?

Prepositions are words that show the POSITION or LOCATION of things.

Common Prepositions:

IN
ON
UNDER
BEHIND
NEXT TO
BETWEEN
OVER
IN FRONT OF

🎃 Halloween Examples 🎃

🎃🪑 The pumpkin is ON the table.
👻🚪 The ghost is BEHIND the door.
🐱🪑 The cat is UNDER the chair.

👀 Look and Complete! 👀

🕷️🧱

The spider is ____ the wall.

(Answer: ON)

🦇🏰

(Answer: OVER / ABOVE)

Prepositions – Halloween Edition

Definition:

Prepositions show where something is.

Common Prepositions:

in, on, under, behind, next to, between, over, in front of

Example Sentences:

  • The cat is on the pumpkin.
  • The ghost is behind the door.
  • The spider is under the table.
